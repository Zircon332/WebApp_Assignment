  1. Description

    The Etude Model EFL-100 is a student grade flute thats the best for used for beginner practice and more so for the apprentice flute player. It contains an offset G, C Foot, which is an excellent choice for beginners as it is easier for them to grip and play without shifting too much, creating goof postures. The EFL-100 by right, is made for people with smaller hands, but can be played by anyone who wants to. It features a silver-plated body and foot for beauty as well as durability and corrosion resistance.

  2. Specifications
    Name: Etude Model EFL-100
    Key: C
    Finishing: Silver-plated, for both body and foot
    Key Type: Closed Hole
    Key System: Offset G
    Cleaning Kit: Included
    Pricing: RM60 per month
  3. Pricing Explanation

    Being a flute, and a student one at that, the renting price of the Etude Model EFL-100 is quite low compared to other instruments. Of course, the model of the flute also comes into play. The Etude Model EFL-100 is one of the more cheaper flutes available in the market, so much so that people usually use it as a second flute for marching purposes. Nevertheless, the flute is of quite nice quality. The pricing of the flute is around USD160, or RM655 when converted.
