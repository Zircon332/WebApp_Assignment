  1. Description

    With elements from professional and custom alto saxophone models, the YAS-26 is a beginner level alto saxophone that has the ability to deliver clear intonations and tones in the right hands and would help develop young muscians towards the path of success. This particular student model contains an improved low B to C# connection that ensures the consistent closing of the low C# key and promotes a clear response from the notes, as found in other more professional saxophones.

  2. Specifications
    Name: YAS-26
    Key: Eb
    Finishing: Laquer with nickle-plated keys
    Material: All Yellow Brass
    Padding: Polyster
    Mouthpiece: 4C
    Pricing: RM420 per month
  3. Pricing Explanation

    Saxophones are no doubt one of the more complicated instruments in the world, with specific designs and builds to keep them fuctional. Eventhough the YAS-26 is a student grade, it can still do the job at professional level, and at the base price of USD2165 or RM9000 after conversion, the price of RM420 a month does not seem too much to swallow.
