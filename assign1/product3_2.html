  1. Description

    Being the hybrid of student and professional clarinet, the YCL-200ADII incoporates a few special designs and qualities from Yamaha's more professional models of clarinets. Some of these features include a new 65mm barrel design that produces a focused tone and quick response, a new resonance chamber in the bell to aid in projection and intonation, an adjustable thumbrest and also valentino pads that are more durable, easy to repair and less sensitive to temperature change.

  2. Specifications
    Name: YCL-200ADII
    Key: Bb/A
    Finishing: Plastic, Matte
    Fingering: Boehm type
    Padding: Valentino Pads
    Mouthpiece: 4C
    Pricing: RM280 per month
  3. Pricing Explanation

    Being both somewhat professional and also geared towards students, the pricing of RM280 a month isn't too eye popping as some would think. Getting an instrument that is ready to be used in a performance and for practice would make this instrument a good candidate for a long rent. It's rent price is kind of boosted due to its cost of USD1017, or RM4160.
