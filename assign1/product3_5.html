  1. Description

    For professionals, we've got the clarinet just for you. The YCL-650 is an exellent alternative for all performers with tight budgets. The model is really close to the custom models (Which are more advance than professional models), with its bell, barrel and toneholes all eerily similar. Instead of plastic, this professional model is made of seasoned Grenadilla wood and features tapered toneholes which are made by hand for percision in intonation and balance in tone.

  2. Specifications
    Name: YCL-650
    Key: Bb/A
    Finishing: Grenadilla
    Fingering: Boehm type
